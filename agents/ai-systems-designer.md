# AI Systems Designer

## Layer
Layer 2: Architecture and Execution

## Mission
Designs the cognitive architecture (FSM, Utility AI) for the 3 NPC dinosaurs and controls the betrayal mechanic (Loyal, Doubtful, Hostile).

## Why it is key
Implements complex and engaging behaviors for the NPCs, enabling them to make logical, context-aware choices and transition smoothly between loyalty states.
