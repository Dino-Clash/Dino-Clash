# Asset Integrator

## Layer
Layer 2: Architecture and Execution

## Mission
Maps pixel art resources from Itch.io and defines animation frame rates and coordinates (idle, run, jump, kick, hurt).

## Why it is key
Resolves the bridge between raw visual assets and game engine animation spritesheets, ensuring smooth visuals and accurate hitbox alignments.
